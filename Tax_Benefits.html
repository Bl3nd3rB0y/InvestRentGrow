Discover the Best Tax Benefits for Your Financial Future

Primary Residence and Capital Gains Tax Benefits

  • Primary Residence Exemption (Capital Gains Tax Relief): The first R2 million of capital gains on the sale of a primary residence is exempt from CGT.
  • Estate Duty Benefits: Property structuring, such as using trusts, can minimize estate duty on inheritance.

Rental Income and Operating Expense Deductions

  • Rental Income Tax Deductions: Allowable deductions include interest on bond, maintenance, rates and taxes, insurance, agent commissions, and more.
  • Home Office Deductions: If part of the property is used for business purposes, related costs can be deducted.
  • Depreciation on Furnished Properties: Depreciation on furniture and fittings used in rental properties can reduce taxable income.

Investment Incentives for Property Owners

  • Section 13sex Tax Deduction (Residential Units): A 5% annual deduction on property cost for landlords with five or more units.
  • Transfer Duty Exemptions: No transfer duty on properties under R1 million, with sliding scale calculations for higher-value properties.
  • Energy Efficiency Deductions: Potential tax benefits for installing solar or energy-efficient systems.

Special Tax Considerations for Trusts and Companies

  • Tax Benefits for Trusts and Companies Owning Residential Property: Opportunities for estate planning, tax optimization, and flexibility in property ownership.
  • VAT on Residential Property: Generally, VAT does not apply to residential property transactions unless the seller is VAT-registered, but transfer duty is applicable.
